FAQ

Common questions

How do customers pay?

They open the office payment link, enter an amount, and complete payment in Cash App. The office dashboard updates when the payment settles.

Can each office have its own staff login?

Yes. Admins create usernames and one-time passwords per office. Staff use the Office Portal at `/dashboard`.

How do platform fees work?

You set a fee percentage per office. Available payout balance shows the office share after that fee — for example, 15% fee on $100 leaves $85 available.

Are payouts automatic for every office?

No. Payouts are opt-in per office. Only locations you enable can withdraw, and they cannot withdraw more than available balance.
